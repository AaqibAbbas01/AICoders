Comprehensive Course Syllabus

Master the art of creating and testing modern software applications, leveraging AI to supercharge your development and testing capabilities. From building robust applications to implementing cutting-edge automation - embrace the future of tech with AI as your co-pilot.

Minimum Requirements

To enroll in this comprehensive course, you should have one of the following educational backgrounds:

  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in any field
  • Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)
  • Master of Computer Applications (MCA)
  • Any related Computer Science field

Note: While these are the minimum requirements, we welcome students from diverse backgrounds who are passionate about learning automation and AI integration.
